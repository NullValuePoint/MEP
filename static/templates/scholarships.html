The CERT Scholars Fund

Deadline: September 15, 2014

Majors: CE,ME

This scholarship is for American Indians who are majoring in business, engineering, science, math, computer technology, or a related field. To qualify for this scholarship, applicants must have been selected and/or successfully participated in one of the following CERT education programs: TRIBES Program, CERT Intern Program, or CERT Scholars Program. Applicants must be full-time college undergraduate or graduate students and maintain a 2.5 GPA.
